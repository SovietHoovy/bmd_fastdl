Objective:
After one team captures all of the control points the alarm will sound and the intelligence of the opposing team will be unlocked. You then have 5 minutes to capture the intelligence. The drop of point is located in front of the gate of your own intelligence.

Other Notes:
When all the contol points are captured they will be locked.
